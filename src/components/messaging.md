# Autonomous Intelligence

Radical abundance will not be created by agents that *know* more, but by agents that *do* more. Scaling laws have solved intelligence, but autonomy remains largely untouched.

Factories, power grids, supply chains, rockets; these are not built from prompts and codebases. They come from exploring the real world, building relationships, managing complexity, and operating reliably. Intelligent models alone are not enough to build a future of high automation.

We are building systems that can manage complex projects end to end. Currently, we're focused on making an agent that can [X]. Our work combines long-horizon training with new model architectures, frontier benchmarks, and real customer deployments.

These systems will dramatically accelerate humanity towards an era of radical abundance. Agents will begin to re-industrialize America, robotics will rely on long-horizon learning and reasoning to operate, and supply chains will converge to perfect effeciency.
